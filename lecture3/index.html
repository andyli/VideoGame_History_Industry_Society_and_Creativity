Ludologist: Video games don’t need fiction.

  • Narrative is not included in the definition of game.
  • Game without a story can be interesting.
  • Game with a good story can be terrible.

Narratologists: A video game is a way to tell a story.

  • Story being told interactively can be more interesting
    (than by books).
  • A good story can enhance a game.
